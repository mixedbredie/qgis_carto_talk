How we got there

  • Strip out the backdrop colour codes
  • Convert to greyscale RGB
  • R x 0.2126 + G x 0.7152 + B x 0.0722 = Y
  • The new colour is RGB(Y,Y,Y)
  • Preserves luminosity and visual hierarchy
  • Optionally convert RGB to HEX
  • hex.colorrrs.com
